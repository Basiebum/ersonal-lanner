About

Built to
Engineer.

I'm Bastiaan — a 21-year-old automotive engineering student in my final bachelor year, with a strong foundation in mechanical engineering. CAD, FEM and CFD are where I do my best work. Complimented by hands-on practical experience.

Currently completing my thesis internship at Hyster-Yale, applying structural analysis and design to real industrial challenges. Outside of the office I work part-time as a car mechanic at Du Pied Classic Cars, working on classic and modern cars.

Two years on the HAN Formula Student team (of which one) as Chief Chassis & Bodywork sharpened my ability to work under pressure, lead a small team, and deliver engineering that actually performs on track.

When I'm not doing engineering work I like to run, cycle and workout. Next to my passion for sports I also enjoy meeting new people and learning new things. I like to think of myself as a curious and open-minded person, always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow.
